Easy Cheesy Mozza Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti, a handful of it
  • much of Spaghetti Sauce but not so much it gross
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese as much as you like
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese as much as you have
  • Fresh Herbs for garnish, so just enough
  • And optional ground beefs, sausages, meatballs, chicken or whatever you love

Direction

  1. Prepare the sauce.
  2. Prepare the beef or your favorite toppings.
  3. Time to cook the Spaghetti with your preferred texture preferences, longer for a more squishy pasta, shorter for firmer paster, around 8-10 minutes for medium texture.
  4. Now spread the sauce generously as you like
  5. Sprinkle those Parmesan on top of it
  6. Spread MORE MOZZAS on top of it!
  7. Place them all into the oven for a minutes and half
  8. With Melted mozzas and a bit brown, your mozzaghetti is ready. But be careful, it is hot and addicting!
