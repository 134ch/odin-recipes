Simply Potato and Mozzarella

Ingredients

  • Two ounces of Gold Potatoes, quartered
  • Two tablespoons of extra-virgin Olive oil
  • One teaspoon of garlic powder
  • One teaspoon of dried oregano
  • One teaspoon of Salt
  • Two tablespoons of Freshly ground black pepper
  • Two cups of Shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • One cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • One teaspoon of fresh parsley for garnish
  • One teaspoon of red pepper flakes for garnish too

Direction

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Prepare food-grade aluminium foil about 10" long, about four pieces are enough
  3. In a large bowl, toss those potatoes with olive oil, garlic powder, and oregano then season them with salt and pepper, the black ones, no racist intended
  4. Divide the potatoes and conquer them, fold the foil crosswise to completely cover the potatoes then roll the top and bottom edge to seal them closed
  5. Place the foil packets on the grill or in oven for about 15 minutes
  6. Unwrap those packets then sprinkle Parmesan and Spread MORE MOZZAS on top of it! then foil them back.
  7. Cook them until those cheese are melty or about 3 to 5 minutes
  8. They are ready to go, AFTER you top them with parsley and pepper, the asian ones the red ones
  9. Enjoy! and sorry for my bad jokes, and FYI, I'm Asian
